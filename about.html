About DeliriumRates

Welcome to DeliriumRates — a personal collection of 100 movies that I, the creator, absolutely love. There’s no critic’s system, no Hollywood ranking, and no list from best to worst. These are simply 100 films I believe every human should experience before they die.

The list stretches across decades, genres, and styles — from underground indies to cinematic masterpieces. It’s not about perfection or fame; it’s about impact. The stories that linger in your head, the characters you still think about years later, the kind of movies that make you feel alive for a moment longer. That’s what this site is about.
