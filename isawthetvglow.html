I Saw The T.V Glow

I Saw the TV Glow is worth watching for its unique exploration of identity, nostalgia, and the power of media, particularly within the context of the LGBTQ+ experience. It's a slow-burn, atmospheric film with a distinctive visual style that aims to immerse viewers in its world. If you appreciate films that blend horror and drama, play with reality and perception, and offer a challenging, thought-provoking experience, then this movie is worth considering.
